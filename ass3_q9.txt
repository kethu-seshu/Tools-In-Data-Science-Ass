In a realm where choices held great power, two guiding spirits, Go and Stop, helped the villagers make important decisions. One day, the village elder faced a challenge. A hidden treasure was rumored to lie beyond the Dark Forest, but no one had dared to venture there. The elder turned to Go and Stop. "Should we seek the treasure?" Go, brimming with excitement, declared, "Yes! We must go and claim it! Great rewards await those who are brave!" Stop, however, hesitated. "We should consider the risks. Danger might lurk in the shadows." The elder listened carefully but knew that courage often led to greatness. Now, tell me, who was the spirit that said, "Yes! We must go and claim it!"?
